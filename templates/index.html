Notebook model

Scansion Physics: ΔC, F, τ, Ω, θ, β, γ

This view implements the handwritten cadence model: word → syllables/stresses → beat phase θ → force/torsion/spin → cadence delta → flow advice. It uses the uploaded notebook image as the reference map and combines it with the lyric, meter, rhyme, and optional beat analysis.

Implemented from the page

  • ΔC tracks cadence change from line to line.
  • F measures accent force from stress, content weight, and rhyme landing.
  • τ measures off-grid torsion/syncopation against the 1-e-&-a grid.
  • Ω measures phonetic spin from internal rhyme and alliteration loops.
  • θ / β / γ tie syllables to beat phase, bar range, and rhyme family.
